Privacy Policy

Wow Games Ltd.

Effective Date: 02/20/2026

1. Overview

Wow Games Ltd. (“Wow Games”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) develops and publishes mini-games that operate exclusively within the TikTok platform environment.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when users interact with our games through TikTok.

2. Platform-Controlled Data Processing

Our games function entirely within TikTok’s infrastructure using TikTok’s official developer SDK and platform services.

Wow Games Ltd.:

  • does not independently collect personal data from users;
  • does not store user personal information on company servers;
  • does not process or control TikTok user data outside the TikTok platform.

All collection, processing, storage, and protection of user data are performed by TikTok/ByteDance in accordance with TikTok’s Privacy Policy.

Users should refer to TikTok’s Privacy Policy for detailed information about how personal data is handled.

3. Limited Access to Aggregated Information

Wow Games Ltd. may access aggregated and anonymized analytics provided by TikTok developer tools solely for:

  • service performance evaluation
  • advertising optimization
  • gameplay analytics

Such information:

  • does not identify individual users,
  • does not contain personally identifiable information (PII),
  • cannot be used to re-identify users.

4. No Independent Tracking Technologies

Wow Games Ltd. does not deploy independent cookies, trackers, or third-party data collection technologies outside TikTok’s platform environment.

Any tracking technologies used within the service are controlled and operated by TikTok.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Wow Games Ltd. does not maintain infrastructure for storing TikTok user data.

User information remains within TikTok systems and is retained according to TikTok’s internal data retention policies.

Upon termination of our developer relationship with TikTok, Wow Games Ltd. retains no TikTok user data.

6. Data Access Controls

Access to TikTok developer dashboards is:

  • restricted to authorized U.S.-based personnel,
  • protected by platform security controls, including multi-factor authentication (MFA),
  • limited according to operational necessity.

Wow Games Ltd. does not grant third parties access to TikTok systems or user data.

7. International Data Handling

Wow Games Ltd. does not transfer, export, or independently process TikTok user personal data across international borders.

All sensitive data handling remains within TikTok-controlled environments.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our games are distributed through TikTok and follow TikTok platform policies regarding age eligibility and child protection.

Wow Games Ltd. does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Security Responsibility Model

Security protections applicable to user data — including encryption, storage safeguards, and access protection — are implemented and maintained by TikTok infrastructure.

Wow Games Ltd. operates under a platform-dependent security model, relying on TikTok’s managed security environment.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
